Protective Measures

We show you how to protect yourself and others from the widespread of COVID-19.
Follow the instructions below to ensure safety and reduce the effects of the sympotms

Get vaccinated whenever you have the chance!

Wear masks and keep physical distance

Clean your hands frequently

Cover your mouth and nose with a bent elbow or tissue when you cough

Avoid closed, crowded and close contact areas

Isolate yourself if you feel having COVID-19 symptoms and get tested

All the above and much more can be found on WHO Website.
